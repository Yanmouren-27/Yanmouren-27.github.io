About Me

I am an undergraduate student in Computer Science and Technology at East China Normal University (ECNU), currently focusing on Data Visualization and Visual Analytics.

I am interested in using interactive visualization to help people understand and analyze complex systems, especially LLM-based agentic systems. My research paper InconLens is currently under review at IEEE VIS.

In my spare time, I actively contribute to open-source projects and continue improving my full-stack development and system design skills.
